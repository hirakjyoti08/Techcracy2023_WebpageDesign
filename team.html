What is Techcracy?

Techcracy is the Annual Technical Fest of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar. It is one of the biggest technical festivals in Lower Assam. The main goal of organizing this festival is to share and enhance the knowledge of each and every individual in this technologically advanced world.The event promises to be a perfect fusion of innovative ideas and enthusiasm. Extending one's limit out of boundary, one can express themselves starting from competitions to workshops including many more events. Youth get a chance to motivate themselves through lecture series by renowned appearances.

To celebrate the grand success and historical achievement of India's Chandrayaan-3 the theme of our technical fest is Space.

Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar presents Techcracy, the ultimate tech festival in the BTR region. Join us for competitions, workshops, and inspiring talks that celebrate innovation and creativity. Stay tuned for updates and be a part of the innovation wave at Techcracy!
